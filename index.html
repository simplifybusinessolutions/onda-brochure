The Power of Two. The Results You See.

Light & Bright pairs Ellipse IPL with Frax resurfacing in a single session to address both surface discoloration and deeper structural concerns — revealing clearer, smoother, more radiant skin.

Image Placeholder
☀
IPL + Frax 1940
Best for pigmentation, redness, or sun damage combined with fine lines or mild texture irregularities. IPL clears the surface while Frax 1940 smooths and refreshes. A well-rounded treatment with minimal downtime — ideal as a starting combination for most patients.
◈
IPL + Frax 1550
Ideal when pigmentation or rosacea is paired with deeper concerns — acne scarring, more pronounced wrinkles, or significant laxity. The 1550 goes deeper for intensive collagen remodeling while IPL clears discoloration at the surface simultaneously.
The Complete Glow Protocol
Glacē™ Facial — before & after
A Glacē™ facial in the days before Light & Bright preps the skin — clearing dead cell buildup so laser energy penetrates more evenly. In the weeks following, a Glacē™ supports healing: hydrodermabrasion gently clears treated skin as it turns over, LED therapy calms inflammation and boosts radiance, and cupping massage promotes lymphatic drainage and reduces puffiness.
